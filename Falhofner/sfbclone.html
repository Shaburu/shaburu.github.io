SFB Games is an indie games studio made up of brothers Tom and Adam Vian. Based in London, England, we started making Flash games together in 2002 as The Super Flash Bros. and have since gone on to make games for mobile, consoles and desktop.

Marching Order

Take charge of an adorable (but disorderly) animal marching band in this addictive logic puzzle game!

You play as a stressed-out band leader rabbit who's job it is to organise the fussy marching band members into the correct order.

Each animal has a particular condition that has to be met before they'll be happy. How do we know what it is? Asking them directly? No! By checking their social media, of course. What a society we live in.

Read the clues, re-arrange the animals, and attempt to march!
